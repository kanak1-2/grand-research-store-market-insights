 <p>Rising Respiratory Disorders: The prevalence of chronic cough and respiratory infections is boosting the demand for codeine-based antitussive drugs.</p>

 <p>Side Effects & Risk of Dependency: Prolonged use of codeine can lead to dependence and adverse effects, limiting its widespread use.</p>
