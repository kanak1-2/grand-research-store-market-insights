 <p>Mobile Amplifiers are electronic devices that can increase the magnitude of a signal (a time-varying voltage or current) from a mobile device such as a cell phone, tablet, laptop, or wearable. They are usually composed of three main elements: an exterior antenna, an amplifier, and an interior antenna. They form a wireless system to boost cellular reception, improve audio quality, and enhance data transmission.</p>
