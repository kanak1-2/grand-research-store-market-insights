 <p>Multilayer SMD Varistor is a passive electronic component designed to protect electronic circuits from overvoltage. It consists of multiple stacked semiconductor ceramic layers that absorb and clamp high voltage transients to prevent circuit damage. This varistor uses surface mount technology (SMD) and is suitable for automated assembly of modern compact electronic devices.</p>
