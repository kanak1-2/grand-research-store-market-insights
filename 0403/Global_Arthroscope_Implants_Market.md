These implants include a range of instruments and devices, such as meniscal reconstruction systems, graft harvesting tools, tunnel preparation devices, bioabsorbable or metallic implants, and other accessories. The types of screws, fixation devices, and reconstruction systems vary depending on the joint involved, with specific devices tailored for knees, shoulders, hips, and other joints. Arthroscopic implants have become an essential alternative to complete joint replacement, offering improved clinical outcomes and shorter recovery times.</p>
