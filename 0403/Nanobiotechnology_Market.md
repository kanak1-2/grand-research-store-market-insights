<h1>Nanobiotechnology Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Nanobiotechnology refers to the integration of nanotechnology with biology to develop innovative solutions in the medical, agricultural, and environmental sectors. It focuses on manipulating and utilizing structures and systems at the nanoscale (typically between 1 and 100 nanometers) to address complex biological challenges. In particular, nanobiotechnology holds immense promise for the development of targeted drug delivery systems, diagnostic tools, and therapeutic techniques. It includes the application of nanoparticles, nanodevices, and nanosystems to interact with biological systems for medical advancements, particularly in the treatment of diseases like cancer, neurological disorders, and infections.</p>
