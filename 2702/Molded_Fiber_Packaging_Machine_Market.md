 <p>Molded Fiber Packaging Machines are machines creatively designed to produce continuous lengths of plastic pieces out of a particular type of plastic. Molded fiber (also spelled as moulded pulp or moulded fibre) is a packaging material, that is typically made from recycled paperboard and/or newsprint. It is used for protective packaging or for food service trays and beverage carriers. Other typical uses are end caps, trays, plates, bowls and clamshell containers.</p>
