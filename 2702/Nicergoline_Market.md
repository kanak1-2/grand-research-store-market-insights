 <p>Nicergoline is an ergot derivative indicated to treat senile dementia and vascular disorders such as cerebral thrombosis, Raynaud&#39;s disease, vascular migraines, atherosclerosis, arterial blockages in the limbs, and retinopathy. It is also indicated for migraines of vascular origin, coadjutant therapy in clinical situations, corio-retinal vascular disorders, oto-vestibular problems. It is sold under the brand name Sermion among others. Nicergoline is a potent vasodilator that helps improve brain blood flow by decreases vascular resistance and improving the utilization of glucose and oxygen by brain cells.</p>
