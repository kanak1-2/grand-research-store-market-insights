 <p>Modular Surge Protective Device (SPD) is a no-fuse, fail-safe surge suppressor. UL 1449 5th Edition approved, it is DIN-rail mountable featuring a fail-safe self-protected design, visual indicator, and a small footprint. A remote indicator option provides status to critical control circuitry. The Surge-Trap Modular SPD has a high short circuit rating and a thermally protected MOV, which eliminates the need for additional overcurrent protection devices.</p>
