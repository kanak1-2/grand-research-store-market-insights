<h1>Next Generation Optical Biometry Devices Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p data-pm-slice="1 1 []">The <strong>Next Generation Optical Biometry Devices</strong> market encompasses advanced instruments used for precise measurement of ocular parameters such as the length, width of the eye, corneal curvature, anterior chamber depth, and lens thickness. These devices employ cutting-edge technologies, including optical coherence tomography and partial coherence interferometry, to deliver accurate data critical for calculating intraocular lens (IOL) power, especially in pre-surgical evaluations for cataract or refractive surgeries.</p>
