 <p>Modularity is a construction method that involves building sections away from the building site and then transporting them to their intended location. The installation of the prefabricated parts is done on site. Prefabricated sections are sometimes placed using cranes, and the modules can be placed side-by-side, end-to-end or stacked, allowing a variety of configurations and styles. Modular toilet cabins refer to public toilets built with modular construction methods. The modular toilet cabins defined in this report only include outdoor and sold toilet cabins, excluding indoor modular toilets and leased modular toilet cabins.</p>
