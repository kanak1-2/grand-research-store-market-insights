 <p>PET spunbond nonwoven refers to a type of nonwoven fabric that is made from polyester (PET) fibers using a spunbond process. The spunbond manufacturing technique involves extruding the polyester polymer into continuous filaments and then entangling these filaments to form a nonwoven fabric. PET spunbond nonwovens are known for their exceptional strength, durability, and versatility, making them suitable for various applications across different industries.</p>
