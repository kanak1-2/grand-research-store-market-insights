<h1>Papain Powder Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2030</h1><br/><p>Papain powder is a cysteine protease enzyme derived from the raw fruit of the papaya plant. It is widely utilized for its protein-digesting properties, making it a valuable ingredient in various industries. Its primary application is as a meat tenderizer, where it breaks down tough fibers to make meat easier to cook and digest. Additionally, papain is employed in the pharmaceutical sector for wound cleaning and tooth whitening, the cosmetic industry for skin exfoliation, and the textile industry for processing fabrics.</p>
