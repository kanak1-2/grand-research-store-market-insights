 <p>Optical Fiber Splitter Boxes are essential devices in FTTH (Fiber to the Home) systems, offering protection and management for fiber cables. They are used for various functions, such as terminating, cable branching, cross connection, and splitting. These devices are typically located at user access points within the FTTH Optical Distribution Network (ODN). Based on installation scenarios, they can be classified into indoor and outdoor types, with further subcategories into distribution types and optical split types depending on their functionality.</p>
