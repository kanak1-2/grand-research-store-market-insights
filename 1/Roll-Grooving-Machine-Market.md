A Roll Grooving Machine is a tool used to create grooves on the outer surface of pipes, enabling secure and efficient connections with grooved pipe fittings. It employs rollers to form grooves without removing material, preserving the pipe's structural integrity. Commonly used in plumbing, fire protection, and HVAC systems, roll grooving is preferred for its speed, precision, and minimal impact on pipe strength.
