Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) in Cosmetics::Polyvinyl Alcohol is a water-soluble synthetic polymer commonly used in cosmetics and skincare products as a film-forming agent, binder, or thickener. It provides a smooth texture, helps stabilize formulations, and is often found in peel-off masks for its ability to create a flexible film that removes impurities from the skin. PVA is non-toxic and generally safe for topical use but may cause dryness if used excessively.
