5. What are packaging inks and coatings, and why are they important?



Packaging inks and coatings are materials used to enhance the visual appeal, durability, and functionality of packaging. They provide essential properties like water resistance, UV protection, and color vibrancy, ensuring product safety and improved shelf appeal.
