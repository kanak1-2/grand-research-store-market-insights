<h1>Natural and Organic Flavors Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Natural food flavors are derived from animal or plant-based raw materials. These materials are either used in their natural state or processed using physical, microbiological, or enzymatic methods to enhance their flavoring properties. These natural flavors are extensively used in the food and beverage industry to improve the taste and aroma of products while maintaining clean-label and organic food trends.</p>
