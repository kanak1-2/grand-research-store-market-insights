<h1>Consumer Network Attached Storage (NAS) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Consumer Network Attached Storage (NAS) is a storage device that connects to a personal or business network, enabling users to access files stored in the drive from mobile devices and PCs without needing to plug in an external drive. These devices offer secure and scalable storage solutions, allowing data sharing, backup, and multimedia streaming.</p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>The global Consumer NAS market was valued at USD 6,892.20 million in 2023 and is expected to grow to USD 18,064.02 million by 2032, at a <strong>CAGR</strong> of 11.30% during the forecast period.</strong></p>
 
 
 
 <p>In North America, the market size was estimated at <strong>USD 2,160.65 million in 2023</strong>, with a projected <strong>CAGR</strong> of <strong>9.69%</strong> from 2025 to 2032. This growth is fueled by rising data storage needs, increasing cloud adoption, and growing security concerns among consumers.</p>
