These infants typically have low-compliance lungs, small tidal volumes, high airway resistance, and elevated respiratory rates. Neonatal ventilators deliver precise and controlled breaths, ensuring optimal oxygenation and carbon dioxide removal while minimizing lung injury. The devices are categorized into invasive and non-invasive ventilators, each serving distinct clinical needs based on the severity of the infant???s condition.</p>
