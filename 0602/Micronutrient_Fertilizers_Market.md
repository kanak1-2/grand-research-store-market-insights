These fertilizers are formulated to enhance soil fertility and crop yield by delivering vital nutrients such as boron, calcium, copper, iron, magnesium, manganese, molybdenum, sulfur, and zinc. Additionally, macronutrients like nitrogen, phosphate, and potash are included in both single-nutrient and multi-nutrient formulations. These products are pivotal in addressing nutrient deficiencies in crops, promoting sustainable agriculture, and improving overall food security.</p>
