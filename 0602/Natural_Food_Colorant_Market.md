 <p>Natural food colorants are pigments derived from plant-based materials, fruits, vegetables, and other natural sources. These colorants are used primarily in the food and beverage industries to enhance the visual appeal of products. Unlike synthetic dyes, natural colorants are perceived as healthier alternatives as they come with fewer health risks. They are often used in processed foods, beverages, candies, dairy products, and more, in response to consumer preferences for organic and minimally processed foods.</p>
