<h1>Natural Diatomite Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Natural diatomite, also known as diatomaceous earth, is a naturally occurring, soft, sedimentary rock or mineral composed primarily of the fossilized remains of diatoms, microscopic aquatic algae. These diatom shells are rich in silica and possess unique physical properties, including high porosity and absorbency. Natural diatomite is widely used in various industries, such as agriculture, filtration, construction, and cosmetics, due to its ability to absorb liquids, filter impurities, and serve as a lightweight and environmentally friendly material. It is often utilized in applications like soil amendments, water filtration, abrasive cleaners, and as an insulating material.</p>
