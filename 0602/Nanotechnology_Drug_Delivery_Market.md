<h1>Nanotechnology Drug Delivery Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>The <strong>Nanotechnology Drug Delivery</strong> market refers to the use of nanotechnology in delivering drugs or therapeutics to specific areas of the body. Nanotechnology involves manipulating materials at the atomic or molecular scale (typically below 100 nanometers) to create drugs, devices, and diagnostic systems with enhanced properties for improved patient outcomes. Nanotechnology drug delivery systems include nanoparticles, liposomes, micelles, nanotubes, and nanocrystals that can improve the bioavailability, targeting, and release profile of therapeutic agents.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>This market incorporates a wide range of products, including therapeutics that can be used to treat diseases such as cancer, cardiovascular diseases, and neurological disorders, among others. Furthermore, these systems facilitate better drug absorption, lower toxicity, and controlled drug release, ensuring efficient and precise treatments. In the broader context, nanotechnology drug delivery is paving the way for personalized medicine, where treatment plans can be tailored to individual patients based on their specific needs.</p>
