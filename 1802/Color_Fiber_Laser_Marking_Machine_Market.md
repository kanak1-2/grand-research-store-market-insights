The MOPA Fiber Laser Source is a Special Fiber Laser Technology That Can Adjust the Pulse Width and Frequency So That the Laser Generator Has Different Performance. Color Fiber Laser Marking Machines Work by Transmitting a High-energy Laser Beam Through the Fiber Optic in a Two-dimensional Scanning Oscillation Through the Focusing Lens and then Directly Irradiate the Object to Form a Scorching Engraving on the Object to Form Text and Graphics.</p>
