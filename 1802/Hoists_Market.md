<h1>Hoists Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029</h1><br/><p>A hoist is a mechanical device for lifting material, typically consisting of a rope or chain around a drum or wheel lift, and a hook for attaching payload.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>A basic hoist is defined by two chief characteristics. The first is the lifting medium, which may be a wire rope wrapped around a drum or a chain raised by a pulley. The second is the type of power utilized by the hoist. Common power-sources are hydraulics, electricity, air driven motors, and, in the case of a manual hoist, muscle power.</p>
