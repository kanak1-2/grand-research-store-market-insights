<h1>EV Speed Reducer Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029</h1><br/><p>A reducer for an electric vehicle is provided to reduce the volume of the reducer by transferring the power from an output shaft to a differential device. A reducer for an electric vehicle comprises a motor housing, a reducer housing, an input shaft, an output shaft, and a differential gear. A drive motor is installed in the motor housing. The reducer housing is assembled in one side of the motor housing. The input shaft is the central shaft of the drive motor. The output shaft is arranged in one top or one bottom of the input shaft and is connected to the input shaft through a reduction gear. The differential gear is arranged in one top or one bottom of the output shaft and is connected to the output shaft through a ring gear.</p>
