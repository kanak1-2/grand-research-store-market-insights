 <p>Turmeric is a bright yellow-orange spice derived from the root of <em>Curcuma longa</em>, a flowering plant in the ginger family (<em>Zingiberaceae</em>). It is widely used in cooking, especially in Indian, Southeast Asian, and Middle Eastern cuisines, for its distinct color, flavor, and potential health benefits. The primary active compound in turmeric is curcumin, which is responsible for much of its therapeutic effects.</p>
