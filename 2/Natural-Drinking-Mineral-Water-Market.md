	 Natural drinking mineral water is water that naturally emerges from underground sources or is extracted through wells, containing a specific amount of minerals, trace elements, or other beneficial components. This type of water remains free from contamination in its natural habitat and is safeguarded against pollutants. The chemical composition, flow rate, and temperature of natural drinking mineral water are generally stable within natural periodic fluctuations.
