Report Overview

Photointerrupters refer to a device that integrates a light-emitting device and a light-receiving device in a barrel, and detects an object through light shading or reflection. Reflective Photointerrupters is a kind of photoelectric switch, which belongs to the non-contact photoelectric switch. Its principle is to arrange the light-emitting diode and the light-receiving device in one direction, reflect the light of the light-emitting diode through the object, and use the light-receiving device to detect. Also called reflective light sensor. Reflective Photointerrupters are equipped with polarization filter, and the sensitivity is adjusted to better meet application needs.
