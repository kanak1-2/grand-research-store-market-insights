 <p><strong>Concrete Protective Liners</strong> are specialized coatings or sheets applied to concrete surfaces to shield them from chemical, mechanical, and environmental damage. These liners protect concrete in environments exposed to harsh conditions, such as wastewater treatment facilities, industrial plants, and containment structures. By providing a barrier, they prevent the infiltration of corrosive substances, reduce abrasion, and increase the durability and lifespan of the concrete structure. Common materials used for these liners include polyvinyl chloride (PVC), polyethylene (PE), and other polymers that offer high resistance to chemicals and physical wear.</p>
