<h1>MulteFire MMarket, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>MulteFire is a cutting-edge wireless communication technology based on LTE that operates in unlicensed and shared spectrum, such as the global 5 GHz band. Built on 3GPP Release 13 and 14, MulteFire enables standalone LTE networks without the need for licensed spectrum, allowing enterprises, industrial IoT environments, and other verticals to deploy private LTE networks with high performance and security. The technology supports Listen-Before-Talk (LBT) protocols to ensure fair coexistence with other spectrum users, including Wi-Fi. MulteFire facilitates neutral host deployments, enabling multiple operators or enterprises to share the same infrastructure, optimizing network efficiency and reducing costs.</p>
