<h1>MmWave Imaging Radar Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Millimeter wave (MmWave) radar operates in the millimeter wave spectrum, typically within the 30-300GHz frequency range, corresponding to wavelengths between 1-10mm. Due to its wavelength positioning between microwave and centimeter waves, MmWave radar benefits from the advantages of both microwave radar and photoelectric radar. It is widely used in applications requiring high-precision imaging, including automotive, security screening, and industrial sensing.</p>
