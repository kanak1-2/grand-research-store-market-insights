 <p>Dental arch wires are used in orthodontic treatments to apply controlled forces to the teeth, aligning them into the desired position and correcting dental malocclusions. Several factors are driving the growth of the dental arch wire market. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of dental malocclusions and the growing demand for aesthetically pleasing smiles are contributing to the rising adoption of orthodontic treatments. Factors such as genetic predisposition, improper dental habits, and craniofacial abnormalities are leading to a higher number of individuals requiring orthodontic intervention, thereby driving the demand for dental arch wires. Secondly, technological advancements in orthodontic materials and techniques have significantly improved the effectiveness and efficiency of orthodontic treatments. Dental arch wires are now available in various materials, such as stainless steel, nickel-titanium, and beta-titanium, offering different mechanical properties and treatment options. The development of shape memory alloys and customized arch wires has further enhanced treatment outcomes and patient comfort.</p>
