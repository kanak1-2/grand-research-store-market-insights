 <p><strong>Report Overview:</strong></p>
 
 
 
 <p>Endoscopy refers to the clinical diagnosis and treatment technology that inserts the <strong>endoscope</strong> into the natural orifice of the human body, and realizes the inspection and diagnosis of human organs and the minimally invasive treatment under the <strong>endoscope</strong> through the <strong>endoscope</strong>. According to the function of endoscopy, endoscopy can be divided into digestive endoscopy and respiratory endoscopy. Digestive endoscopy mainly includes <strong>gastroscope</strong>, endoscopic ultrasound, colonoscopy, duodenoscopy, enteroscopy, and capsule endoscopy commonly used in the diagnosis of small bowel diseases. Respiratory endoscopy mainly includes flexible <strong>bronchoscope</strong>, rigid <strong>bronchoscope</strong>, <strong>thoracoscope</strong>, <strong>mediastinoscope</strong>, etc. Gastrointestinal endoscopy is mainly used for digestive system cancer screening, early cancer tumor resection, mucosal dissection, biliary tract stone extraction, dilation, etc. Respiratory endoscopy is widely used in the interventional diagnosis and treatment of lung diseases in the department of respiratory medicine, the combined application of medical thoracoscopy and bronchoscopy, and the diagnosis and treatment of related respiratory diseases. Specifically, it can be used in the diagnosis of lung tumors, hemostasis of the respiratory tract, and drug perfusion.</p>
