<h1>Monitor Arms Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p data-pm-slice="1 3 []">Monitor arms are adjustable mounting solutions designed to hold and position computer monitors at an optimal angle and height. They are widely used in office spaces, healthcare facilities, schools, and other work environments to enhance ergonomics, reduce musculoskeletal disorders (MSD), and improve productivity. By allowing users to position their screens according to their comfort, monitor arms play a crucial role in preventing eye strain, neck pain, and posture-related issues.</p>
