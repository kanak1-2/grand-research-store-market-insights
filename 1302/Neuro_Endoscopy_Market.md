<h1>Neuro-Endoscopy Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Neuro-Endoscopy is a specialized medical procedure that utilizes endoscopic technology to examine and perform interventions in the central nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord. This minimally invasive technique has gained popularity due to its ability to provide clear visual insights into intricate structures of the nervous system while minimizing the need for large incisions, resulting in reduced recovery time and improved patient outcomes. Neuro-<strong>endoscopes</strong>, equipped with high-definition cameras and light sources, enable physicians to perform surgeries such as tumor resection, biopsy, and cerebrospinal fluid drainage with precision.</p>
