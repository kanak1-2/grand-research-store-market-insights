<h1>Netherton Syndrome Treatment Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Netherton syndrome is a rare genetic disorder that primarily affects the skin, hair, and immune system. This condition results in a variety of symptoms, including red, scaly patches on the skin, abnormal hair growth, and a higher susceptibility to infections. It is typically inherited in an autosomal recessive manner, meaning that both parents must pass on a defective gene for the condition to manifest in their child. The syndrome also often leads to problems with the skin barrier, making it difficult for the body to retain moisture and fend off harmful microorganisms.</p>
