 <p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Rubber lined pipe clamps are mechanical devices used to support and secure pipes in various industries. They consist of a metal clamp or bracket with a rubber lining or coating that comes into direct contact with the pipe. The rubber lining serves multiple purposes, including providing cushioning, vibration isolation, corrosion resistance, and preventing metal-to-metal contact, which can lead to damage or leaks.</p>
