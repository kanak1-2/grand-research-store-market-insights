<h1>Nerve Hooks Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>The global Nerve Hooks market pertains to specialized surgical instruments used by neurosurgeons to retract, hold, and mobilize nerves and other minute structures, particularly during complex spinal and neurosurgical surgeries.
