<h1>Oat Groats Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2030</h1><br/><p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Oat groats are the whole, unprocessed kernels of oats. They are the least processed form of oats, with only the outer husk removed, leaving the bran, germ, and endosperm intact. Oat groats have a chewy texture and a nutty flavor. They are high in dietary fiber, protein, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Oat groats can be cooked and used as a base for breakfast porridge or added to soups, stews, salads, and baked goods. They are considered a nutritious whole grain option and are particularly popular among those seeking a wholesome and unrefined food source.</p>
